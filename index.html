Deeply Rooted in Quality

BEORVA (Be-Ori-R-Va) stands for User-centric selection, Oriental craftsmanship, and the Glory of China's intelligent manufacturing. Our brand is the culmination of **15+ years of dedicated service** in global supply chain management.

Manufacturing Power: We operate **3 exclusive smart factories** and **12 international standard production lines**, ensuring that every product—from mecha-textured SSDs to magnetic toys—meets the highest QC standards.

Strategic Direction: Our vision combines high-performance **Computing Technology** with **Intelligent Play**. We believe that future toys should not only be fun but also serve as the first step in a child's STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Mathematics) journey, utilizing the same precision we apply to our data storage products.

Fully compliant with **GCC, National 3C, and RoHS** safety requirements, BEORVA is committed to providing the Saudi Arabian and global markets with products that are safe, innovative, and reliable.
